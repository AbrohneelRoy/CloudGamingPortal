HOW DO YOU PLAY GAMES?

A. Simply sign up for our service (for free!) & enjoy the games in our library. You simply need a good internet connection & you can play on your browser on your PC/laptop or your phone. You can play these games using mouse and keyboard setup or using any controller you preffer

WHAT IS THE DIFFERENCE BETWEEN YOUR GAMES & OUR GAMES?

Q.What are Your Games?
A. All games listed in the "Your Games" category need to be owned by you or be a part of the library of the store through which the game is available. The Store is mentioned next to the game.
Q.What are Our Games?
A. Our Games are games which are part of our own library & they do not need to be owned by you. All the games listed here are available for a user on any paid plan

SHOULD I USE THE WINDOWS APP OR PLAY VIA BROWSER?

Q.Why use the Windows App?
A. Our new Windows application features our new software which will give users a more immersive experience as end to end latencies have been further reduced & the amount of data consumed has also been reduced by a substantial margin. If you can support it, we recommend using the new application.
Q.Will the browser version be supported in the future?
A. The browser version will also continue to be available and has also been shifted to the new software version. We are working on new platforms such as Smart TVs & iOS devices as well.

WHAT ABOUT OUR STORE CREDENTIALS?

Q. Do you provide Store Credentials?
A. No, we do not provide any credentials for stores such as Steam/Epic/Ubisoft/Origin/battle.net. It is highly recommended that you already have an account with these services, with whichever game you want to play before you login to our platform.
Q. Do you have access to our credentials?
A. No, we do not store your credentials on our servers. Each session is purged after your use. Only your Login ID is temporarily stored as cache.

HOW DO I MANAGE TIME CONSUMED ON MY ACCOUNT?

Q. Does my time get consumed while waiting in queue?
A. You time is not consumed while you are in queue. Your time is only consumed when your stream is open. You can see your time left via the Profile page.
Q. I get kicked out of my stream after a while. Why?
A. You are given 5 mins to login to your store to access the game. If you do not login within 5 mins, you will be kicked out of the stream

WHAT IF I'M FACING CONTROL ISSUES?

Q. I am having trouble controlling the mouse in-game. What's the solution for this?
A. Please use F1 (or assign a key of your choice in the profile) to control the mouse in-game. Typically the mouse should be visible while in menu's and invisibile while actually playing.

HOW DOES EMAIL VERIFICATION WORK?

Q. How do I verify my email?
A. Once you sign up, you will automatically receive an email from us after some time or you can instantly generate one via the header prompt or via the profile page. Once you receive the mail, simply click on the verify link in it.
Q. What if my verification link expired?
A. You can simply generate a new one via the Profile page.

HOW CAN I CANCEL MY SUBSCRIPTION?

Q. How can I cancel my subscription?
A. Please ensure you do not renew your plan before your subscription period ends. The plan will be cancelled automatically after the billed date.
